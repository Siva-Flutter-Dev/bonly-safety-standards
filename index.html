Bonly App – Child Safety and CSAE Standards

Last updated: 08 November 2025

Bonly is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful platform for all users. We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior involving child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE).

Our Standards

  • Bonly does not permit any content, imagery, or communication that exploits or endangers children.
  • We actively remove any content that violates these standards.
  • We promptly report suspected CSAE content to appropriate regional or national authorities, including law enforcement and the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC), as required by law.
  • Users can report any child safety concerns through the app or by contacting us directly.
